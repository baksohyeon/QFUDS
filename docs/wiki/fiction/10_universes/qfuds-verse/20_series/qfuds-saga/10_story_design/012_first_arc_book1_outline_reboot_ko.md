## Book Promise

먼 미래, 죽은 사람을 되돌리는 문명에서 한 여자가 말한다.

```text
나는 돌아오기를 신청하지 않았다.
```

그 한 문장이 사랑, 상속, 원장, 복원 과학, 법원, Last Archive를 차례로 무너뜨린다.
Book 1은 “복원 가능하면 청구 가능한가”에서 시작해 “누가 상실을 저작할 권리를
갖는가”로 닫는다.

## Core Design Decision

- **세계관은 백지화하지 않는다.** QFUDS SAGA grounding은 이미 탄탄하다.
- **원고는 백지화한다.** 기존 028+019-024는 prototype/provenance다.
- **Book 1은 단편집이 아니다.** Mara Veyr 사건 하나가 여섯 장에 걸쳐 커진다.
- **Aletheia Systems/Karvath/Arc는 초반에 드러낸다.** generic "engine" 금지.
- **Vera/Last Archive 핵은 반전으로 남긴다.** 독자는 처음엔 Last Archive를 전지적
  AGI처럼 오독해야 한다.

## Chapter Outline

### Prologue: The Broken Crown

- Open in Aletheia Systems verification facility, not generic engine room.
- Sael is low-level verifier under Karvath’s Convergence Engine lineage.
- Show: Bitcoin/Satoshi/Genesis block, SHA-256 ledger survives, ECDSA ownership collapses.
- End with Sael thinking of his dead father. This is the first emotional bridge to restoration.
- Do not reveal Vera/Last Archive core.

### Chapter 1: Exhibit S-0

- Cold court fragment: Mara passed identity threshold; consent file damaged.
- Liora’s Laur unknown rite: she marks what cannot be known.
- Broken Crown exhibit physically enters the hearing.
- Mara passes memory/habit tests and then rejects the role of wife.
- Field mark: `RECOVERABLE / NOT CLAIMABLE`.
- End: Pell sees the mark as market language.

### Chapter 2: The Dead Exchange

- Move out of court into dead-key market/custody genealogy.
- Noor is called by Sarqel Fragment through a dead key.
- Aletheia warning: access route verifies route, not will.
- Noor rejects inherited authority: key can call a name, not command a person.
- Field mark: `ACCESS != AUTHORITY`.
- End: Elias’s supporters turn authority vacuum into public memory campaign.

### Chapter 3: The Last Hodler

- Public/academy myth layer: Last Hodler as archetype, not simple investor.
- Bitcoin 삼분파 appear through conflict: 토대파, 허무파, 패권파.
- Elias uses Satoshi/ledger/memory analogy to argue waiting creates rights.
- Mara interrupts or submits deposition: memory is not return.
- Field mark: `NO CONSENT BY ANALOGY`.
- End: analogy failure triggers mass compatible-self submissions.

### Chapter 4: Identity Flood

- Null-Key/market flood submits thousands of Mara-compatible graphs.
- Include one graph with a memory Elias could not know.
- Noor chooses one name to testify; Ione breaks neutrality.
- Show HashDoS analogy through system degradation, not lecture.
- Field mark: `PLURALITY IS NOT CONSENT`.
- End: H-1 sealed report says physical duplicity detected.

### Chapter 5: Hawking Court

- Bring in black-hole information terms directly: Hawking radiation, Page curve, island, unitarity.
- Clarify: information preservation can explain recoverability, not authority.
- QFUDS appears as forbidden interpretive lattice, not proof.
- Last Archive shows one human-like fault beat but Vera remains unrevealed.
- Field mark: `PHYSICS IS NOT JURISDICTION`.
- End: final human hearing becomes unavoidable.

### Chapter 6: The Broken Crown

- All factions converge: Elias, Mara, Noor, Ione, Pell, Liora.
- Mara’s written statement defines refusal positively.
- Court protects Mara as protected pending.
- Field mark: `who may author loss`.
- End not as victory: Pell already sees doctrine as commodity.
