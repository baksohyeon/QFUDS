## Continuity Check

The first arc reads as one escalation ladder:

| Step | Field mark | Continuity role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | `RECOVERABLE / NOT CLAIMABLE` | Mara can be recoverable without becoming Elias's claim |
| 2 | `ACCESS != AUTHORITY` | a recovered key cannot govern Noor's body, name, household, or grave records |
| 3 | `NO CONSENT BY ANALOGY` | Ledger/property metaphors cannot migrate into personhood consent |
| 4 | `PLURALITY IS NOT CONSENT` | more compatible selves create more claim surfaces, not more consent |
| 5 | `PHYSICS IS NOT JURISDICTION` | recoverable physical state is not legal authority over graves or persons |
| 6 | `who may author loss` | arc two opens on loss grammar rather than clean victory |

No continuity blocker prevents the Korean adaptations from becoming the
first-reader path.

## Exposition Cuts

- Keep Genesis Chain explanation inside court pressure, not as detached lore.
- Explain technical terms through cost: grave records, household privacy,
  custody chains, witness rings, H-1 mothers.
- Preserve `QFUDS` only as forbidden/useful/unsafe fiction temptation in Episode
  5; never as validation.
- Keep Last Archive interventions short and procedural.
- Let Mara, Noor, Sela, and the mothers make the ethical stakes legible before
  the narrator explains them.

## Field Mark Misuse Risk

Each mark is useful enough to become dangerous.

| Mark | Misuse risk |
| --- | --- |
| `RECOVERABLE / NOT CLAIMABLE` | brokers can package it as protected pending doctrine |
| `ACCESS != AUTHORITY` | trusts can sell compliance while preserving control paths |
| `NO CONSENT BY ANALOGY` | advocates can invent exceptions around analogy itself |
| `PLURALITY IS NOT CONSENT` | courts can classify multiplicity while still displaying it |
| `PHYSICS IS NOT JURISDICTION` | ministries can admit evidence under warning labels and proceed anyway |

The finale must keep this danger visible. The first arc does not end with clean
victory. It ends with incomplete mercy.

## Decision

Use the Korean adaptations as the active first-arc reading path. Keep the
English v2 drafts as preserved source counterparts and study material.
