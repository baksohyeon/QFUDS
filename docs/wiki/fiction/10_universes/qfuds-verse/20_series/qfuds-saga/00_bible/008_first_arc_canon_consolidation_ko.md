## First Arc Core Contract

`The Broken Crown` arc는 다음 질문으로 닫힌다.

```text
완전 복원이 가능한 문명에서, 누가 loss를 author할 권리를 갖는가?
```

이 질문은 다음 질문을 대체하지 않는다. 확장한다.

| 이전 질문 | arc finale 이후 확장 |
| --- | --- |
| 복원된 사람은 원본인가 복사본인가? | 그 질문 자체가 claim을 숨길 수 있다 |
| recoverable이면 claim 가능한가? | recoverability와 claimability는 분리된다 |
| access가 권리인가? | access는 authority가 아니다 |
| 여러 compatible self 중 어느 쪽이 진짜인가? | plurality 자체가 consent가 아니다 |
| 물리적으로 복원 가능하면 법적으로 admissible한가? | physics는 jurisdiction이 아니다 |

핵심 판정:

```text
The first arc is not about proving identity.
It is about preventing identity proof from becoming possession.
```

## Field Mark Chain

초안에서 Liora Sen의 field marks가 사실상 first arc의 procedural spine이 되었다.

| Episode | Mark | 기능 | Canon status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1. Exhibit S-0 | `RECOVERABLE / NOT CLAIMABLE` | 복원 가능성과 claim 가능성을 분리 | keep |
| 2. The Dead Exchange | `ACCESS != AUTHORITY` | recovered key/access와 법적/도덕적 권위를 분리 | keep |
| 3. The Last Hodler | `NO CONSENT BY ANALOGY` | property/key 언어를 personhood에 옮기는 것을 차단 | keep |
| 4. Identity Flood | `PLURALITY IS NOT CONSENT` | 많은 compatible self가 consent를 만들지 않음을 표시 | keep |
| 5. Hawking Court | `PHYSICS IS NOT JURISDICTION` | 물리적 recoverability가 법적 권한을 만들지 않음을 표시 | keep |
| 6. The Broken Crown | field mark chain | emergency doctrine map으로 묶임 | keep with caution |

Revision rule:

```text
각 episode는 하나의 field mark를 얻는다.
그 mark는 장면의 결론이 아니라 다음 episode의 문제를 여는 도구여야 한다.
```

주의:

- field mark가 너무 똑똑한 slogan으로만 보이면 약해진다.
- mark는 Liora가 세계를 해결하는 도구가 아니라, 실패 지점을 이름 붙이는 도구다.
- finale에서 mark chain이 doctrine처럼 보이지만, 실제로는 `pending doctrine`이다.
  즉 권력이 다시 이용할 수 있는 위험을 남긴다.

## Science And Fiction Boundary

First arc는 과학 개념을 세계관 압력으로 사용한다. 그러나 연구 claim으로 쓰지
않는다.

| 소재 | Story role | Boundary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Bitcoin / Genesis Chain | civilization-scale memory and consent artifact | market prediction 아님 |
| Cryptographic Death | one-way relation 붕괴가 만든 제도적 공포 | 현실 보안 조언 아님 |
| Preimage Restoration | identity crisis를 시작하는 fiction premise | proof machine 아님 |
| Hawking restoration / H-1 | cosmic audit temptation | black-hole physics claim 아님 |
| QFUDS-adjacent lattice | rejected/useful forbidden story temptation | QFUDS validation 아님 |
| Last Archive | question correction and loss minimization | omniscient proof god 아님 |

Revision hard rule:

```text
QFUDS는 first arc에서 "금지된 해석 격자"로만 등장한다.
QFUDS가 맞다는 설명, 증거, 물리 claim으로 쓰지 않는다.
```

| Episode | Plot movement | Irreversible change |

| 6 | Court protects Mara pending doctrine | mercy becomes a new legal grammar |

Arc one should not be revised into a clean victory.

The correct end-state is:

```text
Mara is not handed back to Elias.
The court learns a new way to protect people.
Power immediately learns the same new grammar.
```

## Arc Two Hook

The strongest next hook is:

```text
Who may author loss?
```

Possible arc two directions:

| Direction | Description | Risk |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Loss authorship politics | courts, houses, Veil, families fight over who may define final loss | too legalistic unless embodied |
| Forbidden lab mystery | QFUDS-adjacent lattice origin and Laboratorium Vetitum | could over-center QFUDS if careless |
| Noor/Ione POV expansion | refusal chains and Aletheia split become main route | needs strong character desire |
| Tamas/low-resolution queue | restoration inequality and child claims become central | must avoid using child as symbol only |
| H-1 cosmic audit escalation | compact-object petitions push the court into cosmic jurisdiction | science boundary must stay explicit |

Recommended next after this consolidation:

```text
Full arc revision pass first.
Then arc two planning.
```

Reason:

- first arc already has enough plot mass;
- field mark chain needs line-level tightening;
- character agency needs balancing before expanding the world;
- arc two planning will be better after revision reveals what actually holds.

## Revision Checklist

Before revising Episodes 1-6, check:

1. Does each episode open with scene pressure rather than concept explanation?
2. Does Liora's POV stay close enough to prevent exposition drift?
3. Does Mara retain agency beyond being the ethical object of the case?
4. Does Elias remain sincere and dangerous rather than cartoonishly wrong?
5. Does each field mark arise from a concrete scene failure?
6. Does the Genesis Chain stay artifact/myth/procedure, not market prediction?
7. Does QFUDS remain fiction temptation, not evidence?
8. Does the Last Archive correct questions without solving all agency?
9. Does every institutional win create a new power risk?
10. Does the finale feel like incomplete mercy rather than total victory?
