# QFUDS SAGA First Arc Full Revision Pass

## Boundary

This document is fiction revision material.

It is not QFUDS research evidence, support, validation, physical-source
derivation, Bitcoin prediction, black-hole physics claim, cosmology claim,
legal advice, cryptographic advice, or Level 2B admission. It revises story
craft, continuity, character function, and scene pressure for the SAGA fiction
track only.

This revision pass does not introduce a new external web, PDF, table, cache,
digitization, or product-availability claim. It uses the existing first arc
drafts and the first arc canon consolidation.

External-source handling remains governed by
[Research Asset and Product Workflow](../../../../../.agent/workflows/research-asset-product-workflow.md).

## Revision Verdict

The first arc has a working engine.

Keep the arc.

Do not restart from premise tags, worldbuilding tags, or genre tags. The
interesting story is now concrete:

```text
A restoration court tries to turn proof into possession.
An auditor invents temporary marks to slow the machine.
Each mark saves someone briefly and teaches power a new grammar.
```

The full revision should not make the arc bigger. It should make it sharper.

## Structural Cut List

it. Revision should cut or compress those moments.

| Pattern | Action |

## Field Mark Revision Rules

Each field mark must pass three tests.

1. It must answer a concrete scene failure.
2. It must create a new problem for the next episode.
3. It must be useful enough that power can misuse it later.

| Mark | Current status | Revision instruction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `RECOVERABLE / NOT CLAIMABLE` | Works | Make it feel improvised and risky, not elegant doctrine |
| `ACCESS != AUTHORITY` | Works | Tie it more tightly to Noor's bodily/social cost |
| `NO CONSENT BY ANALOGY` | Works | Make analogy transfer visible through Elias's language before the mark |
| `PLURALITY IS NOT CONSENT` | Works | Emphasize that more proof increases claim pressure |
| `PHYSICS IS NOT JURISDICTION` | Works | Keep as warning label, not legal victory |
| `who may author loss` | Strong hook | Keep for arc two, do not over-explain in finale |

## Revised Arc Spine

The revised arc should read as one escalation ladder.

| Step | Escalation | Human cost |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | A restored spouse passes proof but refuses claim | Mara is not allowed to be simply lost |
| 2 | A dead key names a living heir | Noor's life is rewritten by recovered access |
| 3 | The city turns memory into public myth | families become raw material for doctrine |
| 4 | Too many compatible selves enter evidence | Mara, Noor, and Tamas become claim surfaces |
| 5 | Cosmic audit enters sealed court | no grave appears safely closed |
| 6 | The court protects Mara pending doctrine | mercy becomes reusable infrastructure |

Do not let any episode function as a standalone lore tour. Every episode should
force the next mark.

## Prose Style Revision Rules

Keep the English prose style, but tighten it.

Rules:

- Prefer one concrete object before one abstract sentence.
- Use court grammar as threat, not decoration.
- Avoid consecutive aphorisms.
- Let characters interrupt the theme.
- Keep jokes dry and procedural.
- Make institutions feel lived-in: queues, bracelets, seals, wet shoes, bad chairs.
- No exposition paragraph should survive unless it changes the pressure of the
  current scene.
