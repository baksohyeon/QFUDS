# Li 2004 Holographic Dark Energy Future Horizon

## Bibliographic Metadata

- Paper: "A model of holographic dark energy"
- Author: Miao Li
- arXiv: [hep-th/0403127](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/0403127)
- Journal reference: Phys. Lett. B 603, 1 (2004)

## Key Equations

- Identifies the IR cutoff with the future event horizon of the universe rather
  than the Hubble horizon to obtain a holographic dark energy density that
  drives accelerated expansion.
