# Abbott 2021 Isotropic Gravitational-Wave Background O3

## Bibliographic Metadata

- Paper: "Upper Limits on the Isotropic Gravitational-Wave Background from
  Advanced LIGO's and Advanced Virgo's Third Observing Run"
- Authors: LIGO Scientific Collaboration, Virgo Collaboration, KAGRA
  Collaboration
- arXiv: [2101.12130](https://arxiv.org/abs/2101.12130)
- Journal reference: Phys. Rev. D 104, 022004 (2021)

## Key Equations

- Searches for an isotropic gravitational-wave background using O3 plus O1/O2
  information.
- Reports upper limits on `Omega_GW` for flat, power-law, and other spectral
  assumptions.
- Compares limits to a fiducial compact-binary coalescence background model.

## Coupling Definitions

- No dark-sector coupling is defined.
- Candidate source variable: integrated gravitational-wave background energy
  density from compact-binary merger history.

## Datasets Used

- Advanced LIGO O1, O2, O3 data.
- Advanced Virgo O3 data.

## Redshift Coverage

- The stochastic background integrates unresolved sources over cosmic history.
- The abstract notes relevance for binary-black-hole merger rate constraints at
  `z <~ 2` in joint approaches.
