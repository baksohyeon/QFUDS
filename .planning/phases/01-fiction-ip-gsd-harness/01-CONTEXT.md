# Phase 1: Fiction IP GSD Harness - Context

## Decisions

- The fiction track is managed as an IP/studio system, not as a QFUDS research
  phase.
- GSD planning is used only for larger fiction workflow checkpoints, such as
  universe direction, work packages, canon governance, and draft/revision loops.
- The next story-design task must pass a science-auditor boundary before any
  major fiction draft is expanded.

## Discretion Areas

- Exact wording of GSD planning artifacts, as long as the boundary remains clear.
- Whether a future story task is routed as a short story, serial arc, or saga
  package.
- Naming of phase briefs and templates, as long as paths remain under
  `docs/wiki/fiction/` or `.agent/templates/fiction/`.

## Deferred Ideas

- A dedicated canon/RAG MCP for long-running fiction continuity.
- Work-specific folders for multiple universes, anthologies, and saga arcs.
- A later execution phase for English-first drafting with Korean translation.
