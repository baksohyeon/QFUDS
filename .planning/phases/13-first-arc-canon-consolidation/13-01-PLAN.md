# Plan 13-01: Add first arc canon consolidation

## Objective

Extract the reusable canon, unresolved hooks, field marks, character functions,
institution functions, and revision checklist from the completed first arc rough
draft set.

## Scope

In scope:

- Add a new `10_series_bible/` consolidation document.
- Preserve all prose drafts as-is.
- Update SAGA README, wiki index, and GSD planning state.

Out of scope:

- Rewriting the episode drafts.
- QFUDS research claims.
- External literature or web research.
- Science validation language.
- Canon migration beyond the active SAGA shelf.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The consolidation identifies what to keep, what remains open, and what must
  stay fiction-only.
- The field mark chain is documented as revision control.
- The next recommended step is explicit.
- Repository validation and staged workflow guard pass before commit.
