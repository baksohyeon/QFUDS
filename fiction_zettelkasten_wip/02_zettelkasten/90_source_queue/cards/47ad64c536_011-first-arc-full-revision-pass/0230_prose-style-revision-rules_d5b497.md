## Captured Source

> Keep the English prose style, but tighten it.
>
> Rules:
>
> - Prefer one concrete object before one abstract sentence.
> - Use court grammar as threat, not decoration.
> - Avoid consecutive aphorisms.
> - Let characters interrupt the theme.
> - Keep jokes dry and procedural.
> - Make institutions feel lived-in: queues, bracelets, seals, wet shoes, bad chairs.
> - No exposition paragraph should survive unless it changes the pressure of the
>   current scene.
