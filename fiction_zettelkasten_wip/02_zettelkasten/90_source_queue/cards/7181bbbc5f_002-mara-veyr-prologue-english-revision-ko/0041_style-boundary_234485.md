## Captured Source

> Do not imitate a living author's prose. The target is not a pastiche.
>
> The revision borrows only high-level craft goals:
>
> - a precise philosophical problem made concrete by an institution;
> - a long historical question compressed into one legal scene;
> - a quiet catastrophe instead of spectacle;
> - a final archival sentence that changes the meaning of the case.
>
> The Asimov-like function is question recursion across time, not plot copying.
> The central SAGA question is not "Can entropy be reversed?" but:
>
> ```text
> If information can be recovered, who owns the right not to return?
> ```
